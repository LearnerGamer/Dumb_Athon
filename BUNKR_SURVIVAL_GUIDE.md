# BUNKR Survival Guide & Walkthrough

Welcome to the **BUNKR**. The apocalypse has happened, and this localized intranet is your only connection to other survivors. The website is deliberately hostile and confusing. This document will guide you through navigating the experience safely.

## 1. Initial Access

- Navigate to the **Home Page (`/`)**. You will see a stark, minimalist interface urging you to "Go to Sign In".
- Upon clicking, you arrive at the **Login Terminal (`/sign-in`)**.
  - **The Warning:** Do not trust the ticker at the top. The zombies are definitely in the vents.
  - **The Trap:** Entering any credentials and clicking `[ INITIATE LOGIN ]` will yield an `ACCOUNT NOT FOUND` error. This is intended. You are not in the system yet.
  - **The Solution:** Click the new yellow `[ INITIATE SIGN UP ]` button that appears to proceed to the true test.

## 2. The Trial (Sign-Up Phase)

The Sign-Up page (`/sign-up`) is an interactive psychological test.

### Step A: The Button Test
1. You will be presented with a prompt briefly confirming your humanity.
2. Click the **"Register Now"** button. 
3. *Warning:* The button will cause an audio-visual glitch. You must click it exactly **3 times** to prove your resilience.

### Step B: The Torchlight Form
1. After the 3rd click, the room will go pitched black. A droning ambient noise will start.
2. A small "torchlight" radius will follow your mouse cursor.
3. Move your mouse to search the screen. You must reveal the actual registration form hidden in the center of the dark space.
4. Fill out the "Username" and "Password" fields (any value works).
5. Locate and click the **"Enter"** button. This will successfully log you into the system and redirect you to the main BUNKR.

*Hidden Feature:* There is a subtle Theme Toggle icon hidden in the top right corner. Clicking it swaps the dark void for a blinding light version.

## 3. The BUNKR Dashboard

You have reached the core system (`/bunkr`). The aesthetic drastically shifts to an old-school 1997 Netscape page, complete with bevels, marquees, and tiled backgrounds.

### Activity A: The Folder Puzzle
On the left sidebar, under "FIELD FOLDERS", there are multiple folders. Most are decoys.
1. Click the bottom folder simply labeled **`📁 FOLDER`**.
2. This drops you into a sub-folder that changes the screen tint: **`📁 another folder`**. Click it.
3. This drops you into an ominous red folder: **`📁 why`**. Click it.
4. You will reach a dead end claiming "NO DATA FOUND", but a new desperation folder will appear: **`📁 FINE HERE IS THE KEY 🗝`**.
5. Click it to navigate to the final room. Click the giant key icon to collect it. It will pop and glow gold upon collection.
6. Click the `◄ BACK` button repeatedly to return home and view your feed.

### Activity B: The Survival Ad System
The BUNKR is sustained by highly aggressive dystopian sponsors.
- **Every 60 seconds**, the screen will darken and multiple dystopian pop-up ads will spawn simultaneously (e.g., *Zappuccino Zassassino Bakeries*, *Zombardiro Electronics*).
- **Survival Tactic:** You must manually click the "X" button on the top right of each ad to close them. 
- Some ads have a timer. If it takes you too long, the countdown timer (15 seconds) will expire and the ad will automatically close itself.
- *Trap Warning:* Clicking the "SKIP AD ▶" button on an ad will ironically open a new tab to Rick Astley's "Never Gonna Give You Up". Proceed at your own risk.
- Once all ads are cleared from the screen, you will receive a massive flash message: *"FINE. CONTINUE YOUR LITTLE DIARY. THEY ARE ALL WATCHING."*

### Navigating the Feed
- The center panel "RECENT POSTS" displays logs from other users (these are currently static read-only placeholders).
- You can interact with the "TRANSMIT YOUR LOG" form at the bottom of the feed by typing a title, selecting a category, and writing a log. Clicking `TRANSMIT` does nothing, reinforcing the bleak nature of your isolation.

## Quick Survival Tips
- **Keep your audio on** for the full experience during the `/sign-up` phase. 
- **Do NOT trust the `SIGNAL DETECTOR`** at the bottom right of the BUNKR footer. It is a lie.
- **You cannot be banned**. If you get overwhelmed, you can easily use the browser's back button or click `◄ BACK` continuously.

Stay alive.
